What We Do

Scrap Metal Recycling

We recover and process scrap metal from end-of-life products and manufacturing waste, enabling sustainable raw material production with minimal environmental impact.

Aluminum Smelting

Our facility smelts aluminum scraps into semi-finished pure alloy ingots using advanced reverberatory furnaces, achieving 95% energy savings compared to primary production.

♻

95% Energy Savings

Recycling requires 95% less energy than producing new aluminum from bauxite

⚡

No Degradation

Aluminum can be recycled repeatedly with zero degradation of properties

🌍

Lower Carbon Footprint

Recycled aluminum offers significantly lower carbon emissions than primary production

Our Manufacturing Process

1

Sourcing Raw Materials

We carefully source high-quality aluminum scrap, aluminum tense, and other recyclable materials from trusted vendors and importers worldwide.

→
2

Pre-Charging Processing

Raw scrap is thoroughly sorted by grade and origin. Contaminants like zinc, magnesium, iron, copper, and organic waste are manually removed or separated via magnetic separation to ensure purity.

→
3

Melting & Casting

Processed material is melted in reverberatory furnaces at optimal temperatures. The smelted metal is then cast into molds to achieve the desired alloy specifications.

→
4

Quality Analysis

Using advanced spectrometer technology, we verify compositional accuracy and compliance with international standards (ADC series, LM-24) to ensure premium quality.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

Pollution Control

We employ water-based pollution control plants and extended chimney systems to manage harmful emissions including dust, smoke, and chemical toxins. Our facility operates under strict environmental compliance and holds necessary NOC (No Objection Certificate) approvals.

Energy Conservation

We implement comprehensive energy efficiency measures:

  • Reduction in heating cycles
  • High-quality fuel in furnaces
  • Energy-saving motors and LED lighting
  • Periodic energy audits

Efficient Operations

We optimize furnace operations for extended continuous hours at desired temperatures, preventing heat loss and maximizing fuel economy while maintaining product quality.

Environmental Impact

Our recycling process significantly reduces carbon footprint and resource depletion compared to primary aluminum production, supporting global sustainability goals and circular economy principles.
